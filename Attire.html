Attire

What to Wear

Wear the fancy, kooky thing(s) you always want to wear, but never have anywhere to wear it to! Full length gown? Yes! Liberace-esque suit & cape? We'd love that. 24 karat gold track suit? Do it! It is a desert, so please bring warm comfortable layers for the evening & don't forget a bathing suit for the hot tub!
